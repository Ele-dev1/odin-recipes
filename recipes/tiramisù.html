Tiramisù

Description

Ingredients

  • ladyfingers
  • egg yolks
  • milk
  • sugar
  • mascarpone
  • coffee
  • cocoa

Steps

  1. Heat the milk
  2. Place the yolks in a saucepan, add the sugar and mix
  3. Pour the hot milk over the egg yolks, continuing to mix
  4. Cook the mixture and, then, transfer it to a bowl
  5. Add mascarpone to the cream and mix it
  6. Form two layers of ladyfingers (soaked in coffee) and cream
