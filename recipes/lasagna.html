Lasagne alla bolognese

Description

Ingredients

  • ready-made lasagna sheets
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Beef
  • Vegetables
  • Tomato purée
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Parmiggiano Reggiano PDO cheese

Steps

  1. Start by chopping and browing the pancetta
  2. Chop and cook the vegetables
  3. Cook the minced meat and then add the tomato purèe
  4. Pour the milk into the pot
  5. Once the ragù is ready, assemble the lasagna
  6. Add cheese, bechamel sauce, etc when assebling the lasagna
  7. Bake
