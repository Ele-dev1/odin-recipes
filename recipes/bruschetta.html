Tomato Bruschetta

Description

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • A loaf bread

Steps

  1. Dice the tomatoes, chop the basil and mince the garlic
  2. Add olive oil to the mixture
  3. Slice and toast the bread
  4. Serve the mixture on the slices of bread
